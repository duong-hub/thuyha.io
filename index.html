About
Hotels G

Hotels G is home to savvy nomads keen
on discovering the world by staying in
design yet thrifty hotels with strong ties
to the local creative neighborhood.
Hotels G is the lifestyle hotel brand of
GCP Hospitality. A dynamic, ‘one stop’
hospitality solution with regional offices
in Bangkok, Hong Kong, Myanmar and
Beijing operates and develops hotels
both in Asia and globally, with a portfolio
of 19 hotels in major resort destinations
and international business hubs.
